# Data Sources

All datasets are synthetic and generated by `scripts/generate_experimentation_lab.py` with seed `81742`.

The data is modeled on common enterprise marketing analytics structures:

- Experiment backlog records with client vertical, journey stage, audience, KPI, guardrail, expected lift, minimum detectable effect, value pool, measurement risk, and readiness status.
- Completed A/B test readouts with treatment-control counts, conversion rates, lift, confidence intervals, p-values, and business interpretation.
- Source quality checks for analytics, experimentation, journey analytics, and warehouse marts.
- Stakeholder briefs that convert statistical and quality signals into client-ready recommendations.

The data does not represent real company or client performance. It is intended to demonstrate how an analyst can structure discovery, experimental design, statistical readout, quality gates, and data-to-story communication.
